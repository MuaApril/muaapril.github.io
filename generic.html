About Me

Hello, my name is April Powell. I'm a professional makeup artist from the Scranton/Wilkes-Barre area with more than 5 years of experience working on others as well as on myself. I specialize in Bridal, Natural, Glam, and SFX makeup styles, and I also have substantial experience with Airbrush as well. I attended Vizio Makeup Academy where I earned my makeup certification and Empire Beauty School where I obtained my Cosmotology license. I have extensive work experience doing freelance, salon, and seasonal makeup on clients.
